About The School

The school was established in 6th January, 2003. It is co educational school with population of 1068 students, 26 teachers ,and two non teaching staff, Six volunteer teachers and one sanitation manager engaged through Eko Project Programme .One special Education teacher seconded from primary school to teach the specially challenged students The past Principals were Mrs. M.I. Oyediran and Mr. J.O. Onibudo Mr. J.O. Asogba is the current Principal of the school while Mr. A.O. Babatunde is Vice Principals Admin/Acads respectively
